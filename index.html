ESPORTS

what is it?

ESPORTs or electronic sports is a form of competition on a global scale all played on online video games. Players from all around the world create and join teames to compete in online tournamnets and also real life tournaments also known as LANS for large amounts of money and prizes. Millions of people tune in to live streams and now, on telivision networks to watch their favorite teams compete. With these compititions racking up millions in views powerful companys are starting to catch on to the fact that this once small hobby could end up being the next big thing in entertainment.

Two of the biggest and my favorite esports is CSGO and Call of Duty. CSGO is a computer game while Call of duty is a console PS4 based game. They both have some similarities but also are completely different in gameplay and playstyle. Both have players and teams people support as loyal as any other sport fan would do and thats what makes these esports great. I will go in more detail about these two esports on this website.
