CALL OF DUTY

competitive and basic knowledge

Call off duty is a world wide best selling video game based on armed combat set always in a big war scenerio. Cod is one of the best selling games this century with millions of copies sold every year. Every year one of the three developers release a brand new game all with brand new guns and a brand new way to play the game. every developer has a two year cycle which means they all have two years to make one game. since it's on a two year cycle and invloves three different dvelopers a new game is able to come out every year each with its own twist and story. An all time classic game with its own huge community of competiitve gaming.

The call of duty competitive scene is massive with over 800,000 registered members playing every day on websites that connect teams with eachother for money and experience called Game battles and UMG. The main setup for a competitive match is team vs team best of five games. first you play hardpoinnt simple explination its a rotating king of the hill up to 250 points. second is search and destroy best of six round bomb objective game each round you only have one life and you go from protecting two bomb sites to attacking two bomb sites. Next up is uplink basically basketball with killing.
