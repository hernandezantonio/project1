The game is mainly played on a computer and is made by the company valve. This is a classic game like LOL and DOTA were it does not change every year like call of duty. The only thing that changes is the graphics and when their is gun balancing updates the way the gun kill shoots and kills changes but that is rare. All of this is what makes csgo a game people have played for years and will continue to play for many years to come.
